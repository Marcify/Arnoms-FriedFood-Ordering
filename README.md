**INSTRUCTIONS: ARNOMS FRIED FOOD ORDERING SYSTEM**

1. **Customer Information Input:**
   - The system will first prompt the customer to enter their personal information (e.g., name, age, civil status).
   - After entering the details, the information will move to another panel.
  
2. **Menu Selection:**
   - The customer will choose from the following fried food options:
     - Chicken - P350
     - Duck - P400
     - Turkey - P500
   - The system will allow the customer to select the desired quantity for each item
    
3. **Order Computation:**
   - The system will compute the total price of the customer's order.

4. **Discount Application:**
   - If the customer is 60 years old or above, they will receive a P250 senior discount.
   - If the total order price is P1,000 or more, they will receive a P200 discount.
   - The higher discount will be applied if both conditions are met.
  
5. **Payment and CHange Calculation:**
   - The system will prompt the customer to enter their payment amount.
   - It will compute the final amount after applying any discounts.
   - The system will calculate and display the customer's change.
